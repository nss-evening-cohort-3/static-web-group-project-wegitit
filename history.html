Company History


DREAM DESIGNER founded in 1934, by four engineering students in NSS.

The first product was "Apple Delivery" for their roomies.

During the World War II, DREAM DESIGNER cooperated with Red Cross and delivered letters and gifts for the solders and their families.

Since then, DREAM DESIGNER became into a volunteer-based non-profit organization.
