Company History


WISHES COME TRUE founded in 1934, by four engineering students in the University of Tenessee.

The first product is "Apple Delivery" for their roomies.

During the World War II, WISHES COME TRUE cooperate with Red Cross and delivered letters and gifts for the solders and their family.

Since then, WISHES COME TRUE becomes into an volunteer-based non-profit organization.
